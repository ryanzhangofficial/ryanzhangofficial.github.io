About Me

I am a high school student at Horace Greeley High School in Chappaqua, New York, with substantial research experience despite my young age. Recently, I participated in a fellowship at the i^4 FSI Lab at Westlake University, contributing to the development of cost-effective bionic devices. Currently, I am involved in projects on large language models and federated learning at IBM, mentored by Dr. Shiqiang Wang and Ph.D. candidate Herbert Woisetschläger. I am passionate about taking on new challenges and eager to expand my knowledge and skills.

Research Interests

  • Generative AI: large language models, optimization, energy efficiency
  • Distributed Learning: edge devices, federated learning, tinyML
  • Robotics: bionic devices, locomotion, soft robotics, AI integration
